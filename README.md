## What is this?

Satoshi Valley is a farming and life simulation game built around Bitcoin culture. You inherit a run-down homestead from your mysterious Uncle Toshi and discover he's hidden 24 seed phrase words across the valley — each unlocking a piece of Bitcoin history.

Mine sats, raise cattle, grow crops, craft items, build your citadel, and help your valley transition to a Bitcoin standard. Every NPC, event, and item is a love letter to Bitcoin culture.

---

## 🎮 Features

### Core Gameplay
- ⛏️ **Mine Bitcoin** — 3 rig tiers (CPU, GPU, ASIC S21) earning sats in real-time
- 🌱 **Farm crops** — Plant potatoes, tomatoes, corn. Grow over days, harvest for sats
- 🐄 **Ranch animals** — Cows (beef), goats (milk), chickens (eggs), bees (honey)
- 🔨 **Craft items** — Workbench with 8 recipes: fences, cheese, circuit boards, rig parts
- 🏰 **Build your citadel** — 5-tier home upgrade (Shack → Citadel)
- 🪓 **Terraform** — Chop trees, till soil, reshape your valley

### Economy
- 🏪 **Ruby's Hardware Shop** — Buy tools, rigs, upgrades, animals
- 🌾 **Farmer Pete's Market** — Sell your harvested crops
- 🌱 **Seed Sally's Garden** — Seeds for your farm
- 📈 **Market cycles** — Accumulation → Hype → Euphoria → Capitulation
- ⚡ **Power grid** — Solar panels, batteries, grid fallback

### World
- 🌍 **120×90 procedural world** — Rivers, lakes, forests, mountains
- 🏠 **Building interiors** — Walk inside all 5 buildings (Zelda-style)
- 👤 **8 NPCs** with deep Bitcoin culture dialogue
- 🌧️ **Weather system** — Rain, storms, butterflies, fireflies
- 🧩 **24 BIP39 seed words** — Each unlocks Bitcoin history (Genesis Block, Pizza Day, Mt. Gox, UASF...)
- 🗺️ **Minimap** — See your whole valley at a glance

### Quality of Life
- 🎵 **Procedural music** — Adapts to time of day and market phase
- 👣 **Terrain footsteps** — Grass rustles, stone clicks, wood taps
- ❗ **Quest markers** — See who needs your attention
- ⏸️ **Pause menu** — Save, load, settings, quit
- 🖱️ **Mouse + keyboard** — Click to move, click to interact
- 📊 **5 skills** with XP bars — Mining, Farming, Engineering, Social, Foraging
- 💕 **NPC relationships** — Hearts, daily talks, friendship progression
- 🪵 **Fencing** — Build pastures for your animals
- 🌸 **Decorations** — Flower pots, torches (glow at night!), ₿ signs
