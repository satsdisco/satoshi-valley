# ⛏️ Satoshi Valley

**A Bitcoin farming/life sim — Stardew Valley meets the Bitcoin Standard.**

You're a burnt-out fiat wage slave who inherits a run-down node farm from your mysterious uncle. What starts as fixing up old mining rigs becomes a journey through Bitcoin's history, philosophy, and culture — wrapped in an addictive life sim with deep economic systems, memorable characters, and a story about what money really means.

## 🎮 Core Gameplay

- **Mine Bitcoin** — manage rigs, power, cooling, and hash rate
- **Run Nodes** — validate transactions, route Lightning payments
- **Build Your Citadel** — upgrade from a shack to a sovereign compound
- **Farm & Craft** — grow food, build hardware, survive bear markets
- **Make Friends** — 20+ NPCs with daily routines, stories, and romance options
- **Explore** — abandoned data centers, forests, and the crumbling city of Fiatropolis
- **Shape the Valley** — your choices determine the ending

## 📖 Story

Your uncle left you a seed phrase split into 24 fragments, hidden across the valley. Each one unlocks a piece of Bitcoin history. The game spans 3 halving cycles with multiple endings based on your choices — from full sovereignty to community builder to reluctant diplomat.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

- **Engine:** [Godot 4](https://godotengine.org/)
- **Language:** GDScript
- **Art:** 16-bit pixel art
- **Platforms:** PC (Steam) → Mac/Linux → Console → Mobile
